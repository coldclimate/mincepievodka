Mince Pie Vodka

Tastes like mince pies, warms you like a mince pie, perfect in your hipflask when carol singing or sipping when opening presents.

Ingredients

  • Decent quality vodka
  • A handful of candied peel
  • A handful of sultanas
  • Brown sugar to taste
  • A large unwaxed orange
  • A large unwaxed lemon
  • Mixed spice
  • A glass airight container big enough to hold everything

Takes 2 weeks.

  1. Into a large clean jar zest the orange and the lemon catching the oil
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients
  3. Pour in all the vodka and screw lid shut
  4. Store in the dark for two weeks swirling every week
  5. Strain carefully though the finest mesh you can get. A coffee filter works brilliantly.
  6. To get it very clear stand for two days and strain again
  7. Decant into pretty bottles if desired
